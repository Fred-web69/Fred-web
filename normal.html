normal.

A first-person perspective video about the life following the COVID-19 outbreak.



Using first-person perspective, I film myself doing daily repetitive tasks: waking up, making breakfast, doing homework, eating lunch, washing dishes, sleeping, waking up, making breakfast, doing homework, eating lunch, washing dishes, sleeping, waking up, making breakfast... and so on. Using transitions and sounds, I highlight how time seems to get faster and reality seems blurred to the point of unconsciousness...
