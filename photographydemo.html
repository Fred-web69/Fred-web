photography_demo.

A short tutorial about the basics of photography...ruined.



This project aims to emphasize the excessive use of monetization in media, hindering our enjoyment or absorption. Using Premiere Pro, I create a photography tutorial aimed for beginners. However, the video is quickly being overshadowed by a myriad of ads, and the video quickly becomes tiresome and aggravating to viewers. What was supposed to be an instructional video is now visual and auditory nightmare through the use of corporate monetization strategies.
