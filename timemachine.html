time_machine.

Experience the future of corporations as you explore their homepage throughout the years.



This project was inspired by the Wayback Machine, also known as an internet archive, a site that allows users to browse past, historical versions of millions of sites. A thought was formed as I was browsing the website: "what would the internet as we know of today become of within a few years?" That thought became the basis of the project: my interpretation of what the future of the web would become.
