ARTIST STATEMENT


Technology is something we see every day, and whether we are on our phones, on the TV, or while browsing the internet, we are always exposed to digital mediums. I believe that conveying, expressing, and explaining information in a digital sense is key, especially in this modern society.


My works explore extraordinary ways that digital mediums can be used to surprise and amaze the audience in unexpected ways. While the term “digital media” is broad and ever-growing, the possibilities are endless as more and more digital mediums are created. Inside the infinite possibilities, my work relies on the use of HTML, JavaScript, video manipulation, and other styles.
